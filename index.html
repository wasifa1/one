Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is creating something new and valuable.

Sustainability

Sustainability means using resources responsibly for the future.

Infrastructure

The basic systems and services needed for a society to function.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals or communities to take control of their own lives.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is the integration of digital technologies into all areas of a business to enhance operations and deliver value.

Green Energy

Green energy is renewable, sustainable power sourced from natural resources like sunlight, wind, and water.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure integrates advanced technology, sustainable materials, and smart systems to enhance efficiency and connectivity.

"The goal is not to create a bigger business, but to create a business that serves a social purpose. Poverty is not created by the poor, but by the systems we create. We have the power to change the world by creating businesses with a social mission at their core. The only way to truly address poverty is by providing people with the tools and resources they need to help themselves."


Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Advisor of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
